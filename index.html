Looks like you found me. Ok, I'm not that great at web design, so I'm going to summarise myself as a venn diagram:


Below, I've broken my life into a bunch of '-er' words, so have a look and reach out if you see anything cool!
  • Tech Banker
    From 2010-2015 I worked in finance. The bulk of that time I proudly spent at a European firm called Silverpeak, advising tech companies. Silverpeak is a boutique investment bank focused on helping startups raise the capital they needed to fund their growth, or to find a large corporate acquirer if the founders needed a new mansion (just kidding; being acquired by a tech corporate will supercharge your company and scale to a global level).
  • Programmer
    Since 2015, I've been on an MSc for Computer Science at University College, London (UCL). Why? Because I always wanted to, and after spending five years advising people that make cool things (see above) I wanted a chance to make cool things myself. I don't claim to be a good programmer, but I do love it and it does come naturally to me.
  • Tinkerer
    I like taking things apart and putting them together again. I'm currently lucky enough to have access to the UCL's Institute of Making, which is awesome.
  • Lifter
    I enjoy picking things up and putting them down. I also have learned a suprising amount about physiology and basic biological process through years of poring through (bro)science.
  • Gamer
    But not as you would think! I, like any other nerd, used to play lots of these newfangled 'video' games. But, these days I find myself enjoying Pen'n'Paper RPGs (WFRP2E,40k,M&M,GURPS,Cthulhu) and Board Games (Dead of Winter, Eldritch Horror, 7 Wonders, Catan, Dominion) the most. I also dabble in Frostgrave and have a lot of MTG cards.
  • Woodworker
    This is where I start getting called hipster, but I build stuff out of cargo pallets that I find. I mean, wouldn't you? Free wood! Who turns that down???
  • Rocker
    Mainly metal these days, but who doesn't also love a bit of punk and rock'n'roll?
  • Food Lover
    Ok, I agree, abusing the word 'lover' means I can more or less extend this ridiculous naming system I've constrained myself to with impunity. But, I thought it's worth mentioning because I love eating, making and looking at food of all kinds (that includes cofee and booze). My sister and I have a foodstagram, check out kloogfood.
